Empowering Urban
Gardeners

Bluum-Kit is an innovative urban agriculture company on a mission to
make urban gardening accessible and sustainable. We provide a modular
planter box system that can be 3D printed by anyone, anywhere, along
with free 3D print files, educational resources, and a supportive community
for urban gardeners.

Making Urban Gardening
Accessible and
Sustainable

At Bluum-Kit, we are on a mission to make urban gardening accessible and
sustainable for everyone. Through our innovative modular planter-box
system and free 3D print files, we empower individuals to create their own
green spaces, no matter where they live.

Supporting Urban Agriculture:
Empowering Entrepreneurs and
Nurturing Local Communities

Bluum-Kit is committed to fostering urban agriculture through our
entrepreneurial grant program and local initiatives. We believe in
empowering individuals to create sustainable and thriving urban gardens.
